Salamanders are a group of amphibians with a lizard-like appearance, including short legs and a tail in both larval and adult forms.

The Rough-skinned Newt defends itself with a deadly neurotoxin.

Several species of salamander inhabit the temperate rainforest of the Pacificth Northwest, including the Ensatina, the Northwestern Salamander and e Rough-skinned Newt. Most salamanders are nocturnal, and hunt for insects, worms and other small creatures.
